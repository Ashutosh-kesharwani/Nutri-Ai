# 🥗 Nutri AI — Smart Nutrition Tracking with AI

**Nutri AI** is an AI-powered nutrition assistant that helps users track their diet effortlessly using image recognition.  
Instead of manually logging meals, users can simply upload or click a photo of their food and get an instant nutritional breakdown.

## 📌 Overview

Nutri AI is designed to **simplify calorie tracking and improve health awareness** by leveraging AI.  
The platform eliminates manual input and provides accurate, real-time insights into food consumption.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📸 AI-Based Food Recognition
- Upload or capture food images  
- Automatically detects food items using AI  
- Eliminates manual tracking  

### 📊 Nutritional Breakdown
- Calories  
- Protein  
- Carbohydrates  
- Fats  

### 🧠 Personalized Insights
- Smart food recommendations  
- Health suggestions based on intake  

### 📈 Progress Tracking
- Monitor daily intake  
- Track fitness and diet goals over time  

### 🥦 Health-Focused Experience
- Helps users make better food decisions  
- Encourages healthier lifestyle habits
